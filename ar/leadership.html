Leadership Philosophy

Ashraf El Desoky leads with a philosophy centred on clarity, accountability and results. With 25+ years of leading multidisciplinary teams across international environments, his leadership approach integrates technical rigour with strategic vision and people-centred management.

He is recognised for his ability to transform strategy into execution – translating executive-level programme objectives into actionable plans, then driving delivery through motivated, high-performing teams. His 10-year engagement with STC stands as testament to the trust he builds with clients and organisations.

Fluent in Arabic and English with basic German, Ashraf is experienced in leading culturally diverse teams across Saudi Arabia, Egypt and Sudan – adapting his communication style and leadership approach to each environment.

His professional education at Edinburgh Business School (Heriot-Watt University) reinforced his business acumen and leadership foundations, complementing his engineering background and PMP® certification.
