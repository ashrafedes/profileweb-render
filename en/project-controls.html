Project Controls Manager Expertise

Ashraf El Desoky is a highly experienced Project Controls Manager with over 25 years of hands-on expertise in planning, scheduling, cost control, risk management, earned value management and programme governance across major telecommunications, construction, infrastructure and capital investment programmes in Saudi Arabia, Egypt and Sudan.

As a Project Controls Manager, his career began at Siemens AG Egypt where he developed foundational skills in schedule management and ERP reporting. This was elevated during 10 years at Norconsult/STC where he managed the full project controls function for STC's national FTTH rollout – the most complex telecom programme in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Currently serving as Project Controls Manager at AlSharq Office Company in Riyadh, KSA, Ashraf leads planning, budgeting, forecasting and reporting for telecommunications and electrical infrastructure programmes. He is proficient in Primavera P6, MS Project, Power BI, Advanced Excel and MS Access VBA, delivering controls that provide genuine programme visibility and executive insight.

A PMP®-certified Project Controls Manager, Ashraf combines deep technical knowledge with executive-level reporting to ensure projects stay on time, within budget and aligned with strategic objectives. His project controls management expertise spans cost control, risk registers, change control, KPI dashboards, baseline management and progress monitoring across multi-billion-dollar programmes.
