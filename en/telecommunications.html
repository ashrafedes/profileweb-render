Telecom Specialisations

With 25+ years in telecommunications, Ashraf El Desoky has built deep expertise in fixed network infrastructure including FTTH, FTTx, OSP, ISP, fibre optic systems and digital infrastructure across major national programmes.

His telecom journey began at Siemens AG Egypt in external network projects, progressed through 10 years supporting Saudi Telecom Company (STC) national FTTH rollout, and expanded to include smart city ELV systems, BMS, CCTV and fire alarm infrastructure for major national projects including Egypt's International Olympic Games City.

Ashraf holds a CCNA certification from Cisco (2001) and has received comprehensive training in Cisco products including switches and routers.

Technical Knowledge

Technology Areas

🔵 FTTH / FTTx

Fiber to the Home and Fiber to the X – the deployment of fiber optic cables directly to residential and business premises. Ashraf managed FTTH rollout programmes for STC across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for 10 years.

🔵 OSP / ISP

Outside Plant (OSP) – external cables, ducts, manholes and exchanges. Inside Plant (ISP) – internal building cabling and equipment. Both are core telecom delivery domains managed throughout career.

🔵 ELV Systems

Extra Low Voltage systems including CCTV, access control, BMS, fire alarm, audio-visual and structured cabling. Delivered comprehensive ELV systems for Egypt's Olympic Games City.

🔵 BMS

Building Management Systems – integrated control of building services including HVAC, lighting, energy and security. Delivered BMS infrastructure for major smart building and smart city projects.

🔵 5G Rollout

Fifth-generation mobile network infrastructure planning and rollout management. Experience with next-generation telecom network deployment programmes.

🔵 CCNA (Cisco)

Cisco Certified Network Associate (2001) – networking fundamentals, switches, routers and network design. Formal Cisco training on products and network implementation.
