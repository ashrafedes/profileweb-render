A practitioner-led digital innovator, Ashraf El Desoky has consistently applied technology to transform project operations and management reporting. During his decade at Norconsult/STC, he elevated this capability to enterprise level – building SQL-backed ERP systems and Power BI dashboards that provided real-time programme visibility for STC senior leadership across the national FTTH rollout programme.

His digital skills span from database design (SQL) and application development (VB.NET, ASP.NET, VBA) through to business intelligence (Power BI) and process automation – making him uniquely positioned to bridge the gap between project management and digital transformation.
