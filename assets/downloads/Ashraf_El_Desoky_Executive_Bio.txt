EXECUTIVE BIOGRAPHY

Ashraf Ibrahim El Desoky, PMP®
Project Controls Director | PMO Director | Telecommunications & Digital Infrastructure Leader

Ashraf Ibrahim El Desoky is a PMP®-certified programme and project controls executive with more than 25 years of international leadership experience across Saudi Arabia, Egypt and Sudan. He has built a distinguished career delivering complex telecommunications, digital infrastructure and capital investment programmes, combining strategic executive leadership with deep hands-on expertise in planning, scheduling, cost control, governance and digital transformation.

Ashraf began his career as a Project Control and Monitoring Engineer at Siemens AG in Egypt, where he developed early automated project tracking systems using MS Access and MS Project. He later served as Project Engineer in Sudan on turnkey external network projects for Siemens, before moving into project management roles at Millentech Systems and Norconsult.

From 2007 to 2017, Ashraf spent ten years supporting Saudi Telecom Company (STC) through Norconsult, playing a senior role in the national fixed network expansion that included one of the Middle East's largest FTTH/FTTx rollouts. He managed planning, scheduling, project controls, PMO governance and executive reporting; developed an ERP-style tracking system using MS Access VBA and SQL; and created KPI dashboards used by STC senior management and regional directors.

In recent years, Ashraf has held executive-level roles including Lead Consultant Project Manager at Sabbour Consulting, where he delivered ELV, BMS, CCTV, fire alarm and FTTH systems for Egypt's International Olympic Games City and the New Administrative Capital Fiber Network. He also served as Projects Director – Telecommunications at the Egyptian Company for General Contracting and currently leads project controls at AlSharq Office Company in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.

Throughout his career, Ashraf has managed portfolios exceeding SAR 550 Million and directed programmes and projects up to SAR 300 Million. He has led teams of more than 20 engineers, coordinated 70+ subcontractors and reported to government authorities, client executives and international OEMs.

Ashraf holds a Postgraduate Diploma in Business Administration from Heriot-Watt University (Edinburgh Business School), a Preparatory M.Sc. in Communication Engineering from Helwan University and a Bachelor of Science in Communication and Electronics Engineering from Helwan University. He is a Project Management Professional (PMP®) and a former Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA).

He is fluent in Arabic and English and is based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.
